CEO and Founder

Abhishek Gupta, a visionary entrepreneur and tech enthusiast, is the driving force behind the remarkable success of ZenX, a cutting-edge technology company specializing in smartphones and wearable devices. With a passion for innovation and a deep understanding of consumer needs, Abhishek embarked on a journey to revolutionize the tech industry.

2014, the vision became a reality as ZenX was born. Abhishek's unwavering commitment to quality and innovation led the company to design and manufacture state-of-the-art smartphones that cater to the needs of modern, tech-savvy consumers. Under his leadership, ZenX rapidly gained recognition for its sleek and stylish designs, feature-rich functionality, and exceptional user experience.

Driven by his philosophy of "Zen in Technology," Abhishek ensures that every product embodies a perfect balance between form and function, providing users with a sense of harmony and empowerment.
